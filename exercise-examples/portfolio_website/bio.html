Chris Jones Bio

Hello, my name is Chris Jones. I am a web developer with a burning passion for solving exciting problems to create cool things. I especially enjoy working with Python and Django.

From a very young age I realized that I had an innate ability to troubleshoot and navigate most any problem involving computers. When I was 12 I leveraged that skill to teach myself how to create my first website. This opened up a whole new world of amazing things to discover.

Later in life I decided to take it to the next level and joined PDX Code Guild to learn Python, Django, and sharpen my skills with JavaScript, HTML and CSS.

After completing the 3 month bootcamp I was asked to stay on as the IT Manager. I spend my time there wearing many hats. The most challenging yet rewording has been helping students realize their ability to create things that they never thought they would be able to.
